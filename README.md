## Core Idea
Meet your daily quota by shipping the required number of boxes each shift, but make sure you pack efficiently,  your average packing efficiently must be above the quota or you're fired! Items only count if they are fully inside the box, a white border means an item is out of bounds and the box won't be scored. Survive 5 shifts to earn your full-time spot! 

Inspired by how life does not want us to break free but put our minds an hearts on a box. The best example! working for a nondescript same-day delivery company! it may or may not be possible to gain your dreams, and beat the game.......
